Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and fill out the required information.

How can I track my parcel?

To track your parcel, log in to your account and go to the "Track Parcel" section. Enter your parcel tracking number to get real-time updates on its status.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payment via credit/debit card, PayPal, and bank transfer. You can choose your preferred payment method during checkout.
